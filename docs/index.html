AvSpeed Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Location Data

This app uses your device's location services to calculate speed. Location data is only processed on your device and is never stored or transmitted to any external servers.

Data Storage

We temporarily store speed calculations in memory for average speed computation. This data is cleared when you close the app or press reset.

Third-Party Access

No data is shared with third parties. All processing occurs locally on your device.

Permissions

The app requires location permissions to function. You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Check back for any changes.

Contact

For any privacy concerns, please contact the developer through the app store listing.
