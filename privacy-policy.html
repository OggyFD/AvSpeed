Privacy Policy for AvSpeed

Last updated: February 2024

Data We Collect

  • Network speed test results (upload and download speeds)
  • Basic device information needed for app functionality
  • Advertising identifiers for AdMob personalized ads

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide accurate network speed testing functionality
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To generate anonymous usage statistics

Advertising

We use Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob may use device identifiers and process your data to provide personalized advertising. You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings or when prompted in the app.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Request correction of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Data Storage

Speed test results are stored locally on your device. We do not store personal data on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent and discover your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy
